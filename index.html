About Yahweh Charitable Trust for FCS

Yahweh Charitable Trust for FCS is a non-profit organization established in 2021 with the mission to alleviate poverty and promote education. The Trust's first initiative was to provide free food, essentials, clothing, medical supplies, and sanitary products during the recent Kerala floods. All services are offered without any charges or donations required. The organization is supported by a dedicated team of around 20 volunteers who focus on raising awareness about environmental conservation, basic healthcare, and general education. They regularly visit schools, colleges, government institutions, and hospitals to spread their message. The Trust’s efforts cover various areas, including basic education, healthcare, disaster relief, skill development, and treatment and care for those in need. Their approach aims to create a positive impact in the community through practical support and outreach initiatives.

About

Yahweh Charitable Trust for FCS is a non-profit organization established in 2021 with the mission to alleviate poverty and promote education. The Trust's first initiative was to provide free food, essentials, clothing, medical supplies, and sanitary products during the recent Kerala floods. All services are offered without any charges or donations required. The organization is supported by a dedicated team of around 20 volunteers who focus on raising awareness about environmental conservation, basic healthcare, and general education. They regularly visit schools, colleges, government institutions, and hospitals to spread their message. The Trust’s efforts cover various areas, including basic education, healthcare, disaster relief, skill development, and treatment and care for those in need. Their approach aims to create a positive impact in the community through practical support and outreach initiatives.

Mission

Our mission is to alleviate poverty, promote education, and provide essential support to communities in need. Through our initiatives, we aim to deliver free food, medical care, clothing, and sanitary products, particularly in times of disaster. We strive to create awareness around environmental conservation, basic healthcare, and general well-being, while fostering a sense of responsibility and care in the community. By focusing on education, skill development, and disaster relief, we aim to empower individuals and create sustainable change for a better tomorrow.

Vision

Our vision is to build a compassionate, educated, and self-sustained society where every individual, regardless of background, has access to basic necessities, healthcare, and opportunities for growth. We strive to create a world where poverty is alleviated, education is accessible to all, and communities are empowered to protect their environment and improve their quality of life. Through our initiatives, we envision fostering a sense of responsibility, care, and support among individuals, ensuring that no one is left behind in the pursuit of a better, more equitable future.

What We Do?

Service That Makes a Difference

Ending Hunger, One Meal at a Time

Ending Hunger, One Meal at a Time is an initiative focused on providing immediate relief to those facing hunger and food insecurity. By distributing free meals and essential food items, we aim to combat hunger while building long-term solutions for a sustainable, hunger-free future.

Educating Today for a Better Tomorrow

Educating Today for a Better Tomorrow is an initiative focused on providing quality education to empower individuals and create lasting change. By supporting access to education, we aim to equip future generations with the knowledge and skills needed to build a brighter, more prosperous future for themselves and their communities.

Farmers: The Backbone of Our Nation

Farmers: The Backbone of Our Nation emphasizes the crucial role farmers play in sustaining the economy and ensuring food security. By supporting them, we empower farmers to thrive and continue driving growth for future generations.

Your Health, Our Priority

Your Health, Our Priority: Medical Camp Outreach provides free healthcare services through community medical camps, offering check-ups, treatments, and health education to support healthier communities.
